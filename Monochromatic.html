Monochromatic Colour Palette

A monochromatic scheme creates a calming feel whilst also delivering impact. It is a great option for a bedroom where you want to create a feeling of relaxation. The mixture of strong but similarly toned colours means there is nothing jarring to the eye, every part of the room flows very naturally into the next.

How to create a monochromatic colour scheme:

  • Start with your feature colour, this will be the jumping off point for the rest of the scheme. It should be the colour that features the most, for example the walls.
  • Then it is about bringing in shades and variations on this one colour.
