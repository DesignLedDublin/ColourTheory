Maximalist Colour Palette

This is the colour scheme for the people who like to be bold with their homes. More is more is more! It's about putting bold colours up against each other, even clashing. It is a great palette for those that want to create something unforgettable in their home and bring in that WOW factor. This is a joyful scheme, it's about being brave and taking risks.

How to create a maximalist colour scheme:

  • This is the ultimate palette for self-expression. There are no hard rules, it's about trial and error and following your instincts.
  • As this is about mixing multiple colours together, build your palette up slowly so you can make sure you're creating something you really love and that makes sense together.
  • A piece of colourful art can be a great starting point for a scheme like this. Put the different colours from the artwork into the room and then hang it on the wall to pull the scheme together.
