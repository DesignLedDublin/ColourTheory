Neutral Colour Palette

This is the scheme for those of you that prefer a more pared back look. But you need to take just as much care choosing your neutrals as you do your bolder colours. There are more than 150,000 shades of white paint in the world to choose from. Choose a white with too much blue or grey and you could end up with a colder feeling room than you wanted. Choose a white with too much cream and you risk it yellowing and turning into the dreaded magnolia over time.

How to create a neutral colour scheme:

  • Think about textures as well as colour. You might have three white items in the room but if they are all different materials, it creates interest.
  • One small pop of colour can really elevate a neutral scheme.
  • Bring in plenty of natural materials to keep the space warm. An expanse of wood floor softens a white room whereas white walls with a tiled floor can feel cold.
